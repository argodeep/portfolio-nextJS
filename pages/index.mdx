Hi, I am **Arghyadeep Majumder**, a Senior Engineer & Solution Architect currently building **Empuls**, Xoxoday's employee engagement platform.

I have **7+ years** of experience designing and shipping products end to end — from microservices and jobs to React/React Native frontends — for **600K+ users across 1000+ global clients**.

## What I build at Empuls

- **Employee Surveys at Scale** – Own the survey stack across backend microservices, nano-services/jobs, MongoDB pipelines, and scheduling systems, plus the UI, reliably handling 100K+ survey users and high-volume response processing.
- **Rewards Marketplace & Store Catalog** – Revamped the desktop and mobile catalog UX, integrated swag/merchandise catalogs (including Amazon marketplace), and helped drive a significant share of program GMV and higher mobile adoption.
- **Social Intranet & Polls** – Built the Poll feature as a hackathon project, now used by many large enterprises to drive org-wide engagement on Empuls' social intranet.
- **Health & Wellness Module** – Designed and built a wellness module from the ground up so enterprises can run scalable health and wellness programs for employees.
- **Mobile & Integrations** – Led the 3‑month migration of the Empuls mobile app from Flutter to **React Native (Expo + Hermes)** with a 2‑developer team, and worked on the initial **Slack app** to bring recognition and engagement into collaboration tools.
